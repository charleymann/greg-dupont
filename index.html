The Origin Story

The Trust Dispute That Changed Everything

Early in his career, Greg witnessed a recurring pattern: families suffered devastating financial losses because their attorneys and financial advisors operated in silos, failed to build trusting relationships, and prioritized transactions over comprehensive planning.

A pivotal moment came when defending a family in a trust dispute. The deceased mother had professional advisors but didn’t trust them enough to consult them when she wanted to change her estate plan. She turned to family instead, resulting in hundreds of thousands of dollars in losses and bitter litigation — all preventable with proper planning and a trusted advisor relationship.

This experience crystallized Greg’s mission: build relationships where clients trust their advisors enough to reach out proactively, preventing problems rather than reacting to them.
